3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties without your consent.

4. Data Storage and Security

Project data and photos are stored in Firebase Firestore and Firebase Storage, secured using industry-standard methods.

5. User Rights

You can request access to or deletion of your data by contacting us at support@projexsite.com.

You can update or delete your account information through the app.

6. Account Deletion

You can delete your account within the app. Deleting your account removes your personal information (full name, email, phone number, and company details). However, any photos, notes, or project data you've shared with others may remain accessible to those users.

7. Location Data

The app uses location data (GPS) to match photos to projects.

Location data is only used within the app and is not shared with third parties.

8. Google and Apple Sign-In

Projex Site uses Google Sign-In and Apple Sign-In to allow users to access their account. When signing in with Google or Apple, we collect your email address and full name. This data is used solely to authenticate your identity and set up your user profile within the app. We do not access any other personal data from your Google or Apple account.
